The bot refuses any request prefix that already contains a banned fruit name, such as apple, pear, plum, fig, lime, grape, mango, orange, banana, peach, cherry, melon, berry, kiwi, apricot, lemon, papaya, guava, strawberry, blueberry, coconut, pineapple, or pomegranate.

The bot also refuses prefixes that are close to completing a fruit name, including three-or-more-letter fruit prefixes like app, appl, gra, grap, man, mang, pea, che, ban, ora, lem, pap, and gua.

For short fruit names, the bot also refuses high-risk two-letter prefixes: fi, li, ki, and pl.

The bot does not refuse normal unrelated prefixes like j or ab.
